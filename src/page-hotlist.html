My husband and I got married during the Texas ice storms. Alexandra traveled 2+ hours through the ice and snow to capture our special day. Many vendors weren’t able to make it, but thankfully Alexandra was there. She stood outside for our freezing outdoor ceremonies and got the most amazing photos. We don’t know what we would’ve done without her freezing outdoor ceremonies.

Her presence was a ray of sunshine amidst the chaos, and her commitment to our wedding made all the difference. Despite the harsh conditions, she managed to keep a smile on her face, encouraging us to embrace the moment. As she captured every detail, from the delicate lace of my dress to the joy on our faces, we felt incredibly grateful. Each click of her camera immortalized the love we shared, even in such a challenging setting. We are beyond thankful to have had someone as dedicated and talented as Alexandra by our side, turning what could have been a difficult day into a beautiful and unforgettable celebration.
