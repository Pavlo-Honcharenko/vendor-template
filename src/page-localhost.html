Why book?

Alexandra White takes a behind the scenes, photo-journalistic approach to capturing a wedding day. Her laid back but excited attitude helps everything flow smoothly, while keeping her couples at ease on one of the biggest days of their lives. Her photos are full of emotion, capturing the most important moments of a wedding day frozen in photographs. As a firm believer in love for everyone, Alexandra is based in Austin, TX and available for travel no matter what your day looks like or where it takes place. She looks forward to meeting you!
